Cloud9ine

Origial Goals for this Website:

  • It should be able to display live data about weather conditions, temperature, wind speeds and more
  • It should display conditions for at least 30 locations in the UK
  • It should have a simple, interactive map of the UK
  • It could feature couple of other weather services such as world tornado & hurricane maps, and sunrise/sunset times for a list of areas in the UK

Why did I make Cloud9ine?

As of writing this i am currently doing my DofE silver award. I chose Web Development as my skill and decided to spend an hour each week making a couple of different websites over the necessary six-month period. This is my first one. There are a couple of problems and parts that I'm not completely satisfied with, but i think it turned out relatively well for a first try.
